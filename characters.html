Characters

Auggie Salazar

A brilliant nanotech pioneer whose cutting-edge work becomes a target for an invisible threat. She struggles with the ethical implications of her technology while being haunted by a mysterious "countdown."

Da Shi

A dry-witted, unconventional detective with a penchant for chain-smoking and "thinking like a criminal." He works for a secretive international agency to solve a string of impossible scientist suicides.

Jin Cheng

A genius theoretical physicist whose relentless curiosity leads her deep into a high-tech VR game. She is the emotional heart of the "Oxford Five," driven by a need to understand the fundamental laws of the universe.

Mike Evans

A reclusive billionaire environmentalist who turned his back on humanity after witnessing its destructive nature. He uses his vast resources to act as the primary terrestrial liaison for an alien civilization.

Raj Varma

A dedicated naval officer with a strong sense of duty and a pragmatic military mind. As Jin's boyfriend, he finds himself caught between his personal loyalties and the global scale of the impending crisis.

Saul Durand

A gifted but unmotivated physics researcher who prefers taking life easy over chasing prestige. Despite his lack of ambition, he is thrust into a position of monumental global importance.

Tatiana Haas

A cold, unwavering true believer in the alien cause who possesses terrifying combat and surveillance skills. She operates in the shadows, executing the will of "The Lord" with chilling efficiency.

Ye Wenjie

A brilliant astrophysicist whose traumatic experiences during the Chinese Cultural Revolution shattered her faith in humanity. Her fateful decision to send a message into deep space changes the trajectory of human history forever.
